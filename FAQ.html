FAQ

Q:   WHEN WILL MY ORDER SHIP ?
A:   We ship within 24 hours of receiving your order. 10am - 3pm Eastern Standard time.

Q:   HOW LONG WILL IT TAKE TO ARRIVE ?
A:  Typically, Regular 1st class small packages takes 5 to 8 working days. Note that in most cases this is possibly sooner and seldom longer than projected.

Q:   HOW MUCH IS SHIPPING CHARGES ?
A:  Our company operates on the 'Flat Rate' $5.00 shiping and handling charge.(US only) Dictionary orders are shipped FREE. Any deviations in this policy will cost actual shipping NOT INFLATED!

Q:   WHAT IF IM OUT OF COUNTRY ?
A:   If your oder is small and actual costs do not exceed our normal $5.00 'Flat Rate' shipping charges, we will not charge more. However, additional shipping charges will be calculated AT ACTUAL COST. These final shipping charges MUST be approved by customer before we ship.

If you have any additional questions, Feel free to contact our customer support agents during regular business hours.
